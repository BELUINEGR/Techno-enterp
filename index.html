Our services

We offer comprehensive cybersecurity solutions tailored to small and medium-sized businesses, protecting them from today's cyber threats. Our services are designed to minimize the risks to your business and ensure business continuity in the face of ever-changing threats.

  • Antivirus protection and threat management
  • Endpoint Protection
  • Real-time security monitoring
  • Access control and data encryption

Endpoint Protection

Every device connected to the network is a potential attack point. We offer comprehensive protection for computers, laptops, mobile devices and servers using advanced technologies to monitor and prevent threats.

Access control and data encryption

We help protect your data by implementing solutions for information encryption and access control to confidential resources. Your data will be protected both at rest and in transit.

Backup and restore data

Reliable backup is an important part of data protection. We offer automated backup solutions that will ensure your company's data is protected and recovered from unexpected situations.

Employee training

The human factor is one of the main causes of cyberattacks. We train employees in the basics of cyber hygiene, including protection against phishing attacks and social engineering, which helps minimize the risk of incidents due to user errors.

Security audit and consulting

Our team of specialists will conduct a full audit of your IT systems for vulnerabilities and offer solutions to eliminate them. We consult on compliance with regulatory requirements such as GDPR, ISO and others, and develop protection strategies tailored to your company.

Incident Response and Recovery

In case of an incident, our experts will quickly respond and help your company recover from the attack. We will ensure that the causes are investigated, damage is minimized, and your business is restored as soon as possible.

Frequently Ask Questions

Why does my company need cybersecurity?

Any company, regardless of its size, can become a victim of cyber attacks. Small and medium businesses are often targeted because they lack the resources to effectively protect themselves. Cyber ​​attacks can lead to data leakage, loss of customers, and financial losses. Our solutions help prevent these threats and protect your business.

What are the most dangerous threats to small and medium-sized businesses?

The most common threats include phishing attacks, malware (viruses, trojans), social engineering attacks and ransomware. Data leaks due to weak passwords or network vulnerabilities are also common. We offer comprehensive protection against all of these threats.

How can your services help protect my company from attacks?

We provide a range of cybersecurity solutions, including antivirus protection, firewalls, real-time network monitoring, data encryption, backups, and employee training in cyber hygiene. These measures significantly reduce the risk of attacks and protect your infrastructure.

Can I use your services if we already have an antivirus?

Yes! Our cybersecurity services include more than just virus protection, but also more comprehensive solutions. Antivirus is just one layer of protection. We offer a wider range of services, such as data encryption, network protection, and backup.
